How to access AutoOpsedit

To log in to AutoOps from your Elastic Cloud console, follow these steps:

  1. Log in to your Elastic Cloud Hosted account. Use your credentials to access your Elastic Cloud dashboard.
  2. Locate the relevant deployment. Navigate through your list of deployments and find the one you need to manage. Click the Manage button on the right side of the selected deployment.
  3. Access AutoOps. On the deployment details page, click Open AutoOps.

You’re now using AutoOps! If your Elastic Organization has multiple deployments, you can easily switch between them within AutoOps, eliminating the need to return to the Elastic Cloud home page.
